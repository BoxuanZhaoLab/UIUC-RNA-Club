About the club

Welcome to the RNA Club at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign!

Building on U of I’s outstanding research history, including Prof. Carl Woese’s seminal discovery of the third domain of life, we connect RNA researchers across disciplines to promote innovation and collaboration in RNA sciences.

Club activities

  • Research seminars with junior researchers
  • Workshops on RNA-focused techniques
  • Professional development sessions
  • Annual mini-symposium
